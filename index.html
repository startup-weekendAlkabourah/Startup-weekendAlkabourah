About the Event

54 hours to launch your startup idea

Techstars Startup Weekend Al-Khaburah Vocational College is a 3-day immersive experience designed to empower university students with startup skills and an entrepreneurial mindset.

Hosted in Oman, the event takes place from Thursday, June 19, 2025 at 5:00 PM through Saturday, June 21, 2025.

Participants

Become independent

Participants will pitch ideas, form teams, and build startups within just 54 hours. The event connects you with top mentors, investors, and sponsors, offering valuable insights to accelerate your entrepreneurial journey.
